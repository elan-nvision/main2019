Introduction:

Elan and ηvision, IIT Hyderabad in association with Skillenza gladly present to you this hackathon. Following are the details of the hackathon. We invite great minds to come up with innovative solutions to the current problems in women’s safety.

Problem statement

The theme for this hackathon is “Women Safety”.

Participants are welcomed to use any technology to support their ideas and make a working prototype of the product. Unlike the typical hackathons that end up with people working on app making or software development, we encourage students from all domains (including developers) and all engineering branches to contribute in women’s safety by coming up with effective technical solutions.

General Rules

  • Event Schedule: 5pm on 22nd Feb 2018 to 5pm on 23rd Feb 2018.
  • You need to register through the google form provided. Applications cost around ₹100/- per person. This money is only for food and basic necessities.
  • A team can consist of a maximum of 4 participants.
  • Applicants are expected to submit a document containing their idea and feasibility and applications in real life.
  • Participants will have to present their prototypes and pitch their ideas in a duration of 3 minutes on Saturday morning.
  • The venue will be provided with WiFi and power sockets throughout the event.

Judging Criteria

  • Well known personalities in the industry will be mentoring and judging the participants.
  • The judgement will be based on the presentation of the participant and feasibility of idea in real life.
  • The decision of organizer and judge is final.

Prize

Prizes will be given after April 2nd 2019
