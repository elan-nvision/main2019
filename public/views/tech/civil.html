Introduction:

The inception of building a stronger yet supple world for mankind is on and it needs competent souls like YOU. Apart from other exhilarating competitions, ELAN & Nvision is offering you the opportunity to showcase your talent on the stage 'Bridge Builders'. Envisaging more smarter creations with time we propel them to be more effective and simultaneously economical. Now all eyes are on you, to meet the demands of our ever-developing world by transforming your ideas into reality on a cultivated scale

Problem statement

Participants have to design a Bridge using Popsicle sticks (icecream sticks) satisfying the given constraints.


Event Format:


1.There will be only one round in which bridges will be tested.
2.Participants have to come with their bridges.

General Rules

1. Bring your college/student I-Card at the time of competition.

Judging Criteria

Each bridge will be scored in accordance with an Efficiency Rating (ER) which will be calculated by the following equation:
ER = (Load carried by bridge at failure)/ (Weight of Bridge)
