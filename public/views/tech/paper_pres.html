Introduction:

Elan & Nvision,IIT Hyderabad proudly presents its exploration event "Paper Presentation". A out-and-out platform where students from all backgrounds manifest their research work by presenting their proficient papers related to various topics in the fields of science and engineering to ingenious judges.you are expected to send an abstract of your work based on which you will be qualified to present your paper during Elan & Nvision 2k19.

JUDGING CRITERIA:-

FOR ABSTRACT:
1. The abstract should be simple yet clear about your topic of research.
2. The topic on which the research is done should strive to make the human life a better one.
3. If the topic about which the abstract is submitted is an extension to one of the works done earlier but a better version of that.The ways in which it is a better one should be mentioned.
Upload your abstracts here .

FOR ROUND 2:
1. Correctness of facts.
2. Methods of analysis.
3. Innovativeness of idea.
4. Presentation of solution or ideas.
5. Practical feasibility or implication of ideas.

Rules and Regulations:

1. Shortlisting will be done based on Abstracts.
2. All relevant data and figures must be represented in the paper.
3. The Abstract should be submitted in Size 11,BOLD,Calibri
4. Teams of maximum 3 members are allowed.
5. Organizers decision will be final and binding.
6. All results will be announced on the website and will be mailed to the team Members.
7. Add your team ELAN ID and team member details (Names, ELAN ID’s, Mail ID’s) to the abstract.
8. last date for the submission of abstract has been postponed to 12th Feb 2019
9. Any abstract that is selected and is not presented shall not qualify for any award.
10. There is no registration fee
