Introduction:


Today’s world is completely fascinated with gadgets, they make life simpler and work efficiently. IoT is the thing which makes every single gadget smarter than ever before and also it makes them easily accessible across the globe. That is why this is still an active area of interest. So let's make some gadget which simplifies our life using IoT. To achieve this come and participate in this event and show what you’ve got in you to make the world a smarter place.

Problem statement:

This event checks the basic level knowledge of the student in the field of IOT.
This event will consist of three rounds namely,
Qualification round
Second round
Final round
1. The qualification will test the amount of knowledge the student has in very basic level things related to IOT.
2. The second round will check the basic arduino knowledge of the student and the final round will be totally based on Arduino UNO.
3. The final round will be the implementation of your Iot knowledge and the problem statement will be given on the spot.

Event rules:

1. Maximum of two people are allowed to participate in a group.
2. Every participant should be registered which can be done before hand or on the spot.
3. Arduino boards and other required components will be provided.
4. Organisers decision is final in all matters.
5. Participant should be in time for the event.
6. The group which qualifies 1st round(Qualification round) will get participation certificate
7. Any kind of malpractices will not be entertained and the participant will be disqualified.

Introduction

Are you fascinated by electric circuitry and passionate about electronics? This one’s for you then. Plug in and get elektrified!!

Round 1

It is an offline quiz where in some theoretical questions on electronics would be asked.

Round 2

This round will test your ability to understand the circuit and debug it. Identical circuits would be given to each group.Required information about the circuit will be given. You will have to tweak the circuit given to you to make it work.

Rules and regulation:

1. It is an offline quiz event.
2. Team size is 2 or 3.
3. The event consists of two rounds.
4. Number of teams that get selected for second round will be decided on spot based on participation.
5. In any case, decision of organiser is final.

Judging criteria:

1. The team with maximum points is declared as winner.
2. Participation certificates are given only to those who clear round 1
