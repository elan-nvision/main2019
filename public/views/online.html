Introduction

Dive into a world of colours, capture the colours in your life that you otherwise overlook and make them tell a story.

Event Format:

It is an online event. All entries should be posted on Facebook or Instagram The entries will be judged on the basis of engagement(likes,comments,shares-80%) and relevance to the theme.(20%)

Rules and Regulations:

The theme of the competition is ‘Pop Of Colours’
The pictures may have a single or multiple dominant colours
An entry will qualify if it is a photograph or artwork that has not been plagiarised, supersaturated, or used any filters for modification
All entries have to be posted publicly on Facebook or Instagram
All entries have to be mailed to events.online@elan.org.in as well
To qualify as an entry it has to be tagged with #emblazon #PopOfColours along with 3 of your friends on either platforms

More Information

Event Closes on 24th February 2019 - 23:59

Prizes:

Prizes worth INR 2000 will be awarded to the winner(s) The winning photos/artworks will be shared on our and InFocus' Facebook & Instagram handles.
