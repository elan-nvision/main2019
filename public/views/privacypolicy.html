PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is important to us. It is Elan & nvision's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, http://elan.org.in, and other sites we own and operate. You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services.

This privacy policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they shared and collect in elan.org.in. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline.

Your continued use of our website will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us.

This policy is effective as of 1 January 2019.
