Introduction

Do you think you can take after Steven Spielberg, Christopher Nolan and James Cameron? Make your own short film, enthral and captivate movie buffs with your creativity. The stage at Elan & ηvision 2019 is set for you. Get judged by esteemed people in film industry.

EVENT FORMAT:

All the entries are judged on the first day to shortlist 6 out of them.
Team Size: At most 15 in the core team(No particular limit for actors in the film)

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

Run time should not be more than 20 minutes
Atleast one member from each team should be present while the judgement is taking place.
Movie should be shot at a minimum resolution of 480 pixels. Marks might be reduced due to poor video/ audio quality.
Entries must be the original work of the entrant.
Any attempt at copyright infringement can lead to immediate disqualification.
The content of the movie should be appropriate for public screening and thus have no vulgarity. Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
Sound tracks (no max time limit) and stock footage (time limit : max of 1 min 30 seconds) can be borrowed, but with due credits.
Films in languages other than English, Hindi, and Telugu should be accompanied with subtitles.
Participants should send their videos/youtube links of the videos to yashodhan@elan.org.in.
Only those short films which are shot and released after 1st April 2018 would be accepted.
The decision of the organizers and the judges is final and binding.
The organising team has the complete right to change the prize money if the participation is less than six.

JUDGING CRITERIA:

Acting
Script
Direction
Screenplay
Costumes
Dialogues
Cinematography
Sound editing/ mixing
Visual effects

PRIZES:

Prizes worth Rs.15,000/-
Prizes will be given after April 2nd 2019

Introduction

Do you think you can mesmerize audience with your screenplay and make them sit on the edges of their seats throughout ? Then, Elan & ηvision 2019 challenges you with its first ever screenwriting competition.

EVENT FORMAT:

Each team has to submit an original screenplay of a scene (based on the theme/situation) of not more than 5 minutes of screentime.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

ScreenplayEach team will be provided with a theme/situation by the organiser.
Must be well formatted. For example, one page of your screenplay should be equivalent to one minute of screentime.
Participants should submit their work in 5 hours to the organiser.
Time exceeding penalties:
15 min after deadline - 5 marks 30 min after deadline- 10 marks 60 min after deadline- 20 marks Organisers will not accept the scripts after 1 hour of the deadline.

JUDGING CRITERIA:

Content/Story
Formatting of the screenplay

PRIZES:

Prizes worth Rs.5,000/-
Prizes will be given after April 2nd 2019
