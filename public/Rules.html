RULES OF ACCOMODATION

  • Rooms will be alloted from 5 30 pm to 8 30 pm saturday and should be vacated before 10 am, Sunday.
  • No provisions for bedsheets will be made, please bring your own bedsheets.
  • The rooms provided are in the temporary campus.
  • All payments are final, and no refunds will be provided.
  • Transportation will be provided to and from the temporary campus at specified times.

RULES FOR REGISTRATION

  • Offline registrations will be outside auditorium in academic block A, between 8 am to 11 am.
  • Online registrations will also be verified outside the auditorium, academic block A from 8 30 am.
  • Cloakroom will be provided for luggage storage during workshop.

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED IN THE CAMPUS

  • Kindly carry your college ID cards and registration cards (provided during on-spot registration) in the campus
  • Rules of the hostel should be followed. Girls should be in their allotted accommodation by 12 am.
  • Any kind of misbehavior will not be tolerated and will be dealt with strictly.
  • Security personnel are not responsible for any of your personal belongings. They reserve the right to refuse your entry at any point of time if they deem it unsafe.
  • Kindly cooperate with the Hospitality Team.
  • Students are advised not to keep large amounts of cash or valuables in the provided accommodation. The student is responsible for the safety of his/her belongings. Cloak rooms will be provided where you can keep your luggage.
  • Any damage/breakage to hostel property will be charged to the occupants of the block with a fine. Disciplinary action will also be initiated.
  • Substance abuse, consumption of alcohol and smoking or chewing of tobacco and related products are strictly banned in the campus.
  • Water should be used carefully and should not be wasted. Keep the campus clean.
