Dreams
Each year we strive to create an experience that will etch itself into your memories. For all our time here, we have seen. We have seen colours, we've seen the dark, we've seen the world, the waters, the sky and beyond. We've seen people, we've seen thinkers, workers, achievers. We've seen them dream. This is our aim - to give a voice to anyone with an imagination, and a chance to show the world their crazy and creative side. 'Prismatic Dreams' is a nod to those who dare to dream and show their colours when the prism of life stands in their way.

Let your voices paint the world.

Litr
Social Cause
Bound by the rusting chains of a haunting past, we succumbed to the laws of a land from years past. And as time goes on, change is the only constant. Change in our Laws. Change in our Perspective. Change in Love. On the 6th of September, 2018, we all hailed the landmark decision that struck down those laws, but history will testify, that the common man is the in the seat of power. The fight for another Independence has just begun, and we are all a part of it.

Join us as we celebrate the freedom of the LGBT community in India, not as outsiders, but as one of our own, in this melting pot.
