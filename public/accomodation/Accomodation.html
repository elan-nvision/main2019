Accomodation & Transport

RULES

Below are rules that are to be followed that will help us ensure your festive experience.

RULES OF ACCOMODATION:

  • Rooms will be alloted from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM on all 4 days 21st, 22nd, 23rd and 24th of February
  • Rooms should be vacated before 9 AM, the next day
  • No provisions for the bedsheets will be made, please bring your own bedsheets
  • The rooms are provided strictly on a sharing basis
  • The rooms provided are in the temporary campus
  • All payments are final, and no refunds will be provided
  • Transportation will be provided to and from the temporary campus at specified times
  • Bring your own lock and key for safety
  • Cloakroom will be provided for luggage storage during workshop and fest

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED IN THE CAMPUS:

  • Kindly carry your college ID cards and registration cards (provided during on-spot registration) in the campus
  • Rules of the hostel should be followed. Girls should be in their allotted accommodation by 12 am.
  • Any kind of misbehavior will not be tolerated and will be dealt with strictly.
  • Security personnel are not responsible for any of your personal belongings.
  • Security personnel reserve the right to refuse your entry at any point of time if they deem it unsafe.
  • Kindly cooperate with the Hospitality Team.
  • Students are advised not to keep large amounts of cash or valuables in the provided accommodation. The student is responsible for the safety of his/her belongings. Cloak rooms will be provided in the main campus where you can keep your luggage.
  • Any damage/breakage to hostel property will be charged to the occupants of the block with a fine. Disciplinary action will also be initiated.
  • Substance abuse, consumption of alcohol and smoking or chewing of tobacco and related products are strictly banned in the campus.
  • Water should be used carefully and should not be wasted. Keep the campus clean.
